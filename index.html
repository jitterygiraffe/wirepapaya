About Us

We are a small, woman-owned design studio. We love creating beautiful, functional,and thoughtfully-designed stationery that brings joy and inspiration to your everyday life.

We illustrate, print, cut, and bind each item ourselves, with our own hands. When you purchase our prints and stationery, you're contributing to a sustainable, artisanal approach to design.

Discover our latest collection of planners, journals, stickers, bookmarks, art prints, tote bags, calendars, postcards, and notebooks, thoughtfully and beautifully made in our home studio.
