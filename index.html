About Us

We are a small, woman-owned design studio. We love creating beautiful, functional,and thoughtfully-designed stationery products that bring joy and inspiration to your everyday life.

We illustrate, print, cut, and bind each item ourselves, with our own hands. When you purchase our prints and stationery, you're contributing to a sustainable, artisanal approach to design.
